Problem and Value Proposition

AI agents can act faster than governance systems can explain.

Organizations need visibility into what agents did, what data they touched, whether policies were followed, and who approved sensitive actions.

Agent RegistryDocument owner, purpose, tools, data access, and risk.
Policy ControlsDefine approved, approval-required, and prohibited actions.
Action LogsCapture agent activity for consistent review evidence.
AI Risk ClassificationClassify risk using policy context and scoring rules.
Human ApprovalRoute higher-risk actions to reviewers for decisions.
Audit ReportsGenerate downloadable evidence for classroom demos.
